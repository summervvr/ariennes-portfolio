Service Marketing

Scale Model Project

Culminating Activity 2023-2024

For our Scale Model Project, our group chose Dali Store, a brand known for affordable essentials such as utilities, canned goods, toiletries, and detergents. Inspired by its accessibility, we proposed an innovation that would modernize its services and create a more efficient shopping experience for consumers.

“Designed for city consumers aged 21–45, budget-conscious, practical, and always on the lookout for affordable yet efficient grocery options.”
